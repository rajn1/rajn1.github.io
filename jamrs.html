What does our app do?

  • Location Services

    The main reasoning for our app is to provide the user with ringtone-changing options based on where they are. Many a time, a member of the JAMRS has been in an important business meeting when their phone goes off because they forgot to mute it, or had their phone on silent when they were at home because they forgot and left it muted. With our app, the user can ideally set radiuses. If they enter the radius of their school or workplace, their phone will be muted, or atleast have its settings changed to what the user request. Then, when they leave that radius, or enter another radius, the sound settings will revert or change to another level.

  • Wifi Network-based Volume

    We are also looking into using wireless networks to determine sound volume. If one was to be connected to their home wifi, for example, the app would increase their phone volume - if that's what the user wants - because it knows the phone is near the home. This idea is still being looked into, and is not currently in the app

  • Shared Zones

    As the app developed, we also realized that shared zones could also be a useful way for businesses to easily create zones for their users. If a service or group wants to create a silent zone for their customers, they can create their own shared zone, and then, as long as the user approves it, that zone will apply to any users of the app. For example, a movie theater could create a zone around each of their movie theaters, and then when an app user enters the zone, their phones will automatially mute. That way, a movie theater doesn't need to worry about movies being disrupted by phones, and users don't need to worry about inadvertantly disrupting a movie. Win-win.
